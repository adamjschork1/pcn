Welcome to the Portland Coder's Network!


As you may have guessed by the title this is a networking and resource page for software developers in the Portland metro area. This is your free resource to connect with others, share techniques, expand knowledge, and explore career opportunities. Have fun exploring, and if you experience any bugs, feel free to report them.

Whether you're an aspiring developer or have been one for a long time, you're welcome here. This is an open source community that understands by working together we are stronger and can accomplish far more. If you're near the Portland, Oregon area, that's why this group was created. This enables you to find other like minded individuals and meet in person over coffee, beer or whatever!
